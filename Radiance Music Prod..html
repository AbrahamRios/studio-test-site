About Artist:

Musician

Abraham Noah Rios, also known as Radiance, is an American music producer and musician. Rios was born on August 10th, 1999, and raised in a small town called Mendota. Rios produces a variety of styles consisting of electronic music like Future House, Future Bass, Chill-Trap, Hip-Hop, and sometimes Emotional Hip-hop. Sometimes Rios will produce music with actual instruments such as Guitar, Bass Guitar, Drums, Keys, and Vocals to make rock, metal, alternative rock and such genres. As of now, Rios only has one album called "The Beginning".
